Chicken Soup

This chicken soup is made with only the greatest quality of ingredients, such as sourcing the chicken from the farms of somewhere in Germany and vegetables also from somewhere in Germany. The water comes from the sink and the spices are imported from under the harsh sun of India.

Ingredients

  • 200g Chicken breast
  • 200g mixed vegetables
  • 500ml water
  • 1 tbsp cayenne
  • a pinch of black pepper and salt
  • Two spoonfuls of chicken bouillion
  • 1/2 tbsp of chili powder and dried garlic together

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into small strips or bite size chunks depending on preference
  2. Chop vegetables too large for the soup
  3. Bring the water to a boil iin a pot
  4. add chicken and add half the seasonings and then add the vegetables and the rest of the seasonings after 2 mins
  5. add the chicken bouillion
  6. cook until the vegetables and chicken are soft and tender
  7. Enjoy!
